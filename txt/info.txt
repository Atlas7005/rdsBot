***Information:***
Ruby Drift School is an online school for learning how to drift in Assetto Corsa.

***Learning:***
You can learn simply by watching our videos and reading our articles, or you can participate in our regular multiplayer drift events.
You may also ask one of our instructors to help you individually.

***Servers:***
We have public Assetto Corsa servers, so you can practice. You can find their invite links under <#965571491120971796>.
Make sure to read the <#965353705958424667>.

***Team:***
We have an actual team for competitions and external events, to join this you'd have to be a student of the school and get picked by one of the team captains.
Find more info in <#965562051533611008>
